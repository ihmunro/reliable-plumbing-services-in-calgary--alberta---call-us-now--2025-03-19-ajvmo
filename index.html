Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service in Calgary?

We provide comprehensive plumbing services throughout Calgary and surrounding areas, including Airdrie, Cochrane, Chestermere, and Okotoks. Our team is strategically positioned to reach any location within 30 minutes for emergency services.

What are your payment options?

We accept all major credit cards, debit, e-transfer, and cash. For larger projects, we offer flexible financing options with approved credit. We provide detailed written estimates before beginning any work.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency plumbing services with no overtime charges. Our emergency response team typically arrives within 30 minutes of your call, fully equipped to handle any plumbing crisis.

What warranties do you offer?

We stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. Parts and labor are warranted for 1 year on repairs, and up to 10 years on new installations. Extended warranty options are available for major installations.

Are your plumbers licensed and insured?

All our technicians are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We maintain $5 million in liability insurance and ensure all our staff undergo regular training and certification updates to stay current with the latest plumbing technologies and safety standards.
